How to Use This Library

1. Your Hosted Texts: First upload your text files (like aristoteles.txt) to your GitHub repository. Then add them here with the direct link.

2. Archive.org Resources: Find free books on archive.org and add them here. Example: The Kybalion at https://archive.org/details/kybalionastudyh00unkngoog

3. Other Free Resources: Add any freely accessible PDFs, texts, or online books from Project Gutenberg, Sacred Texts Archive, etc.

4. Organization: Use categories to keep your library organized. All resources are saved in your browser's local storage.

Note: Always verify that resources are in the public domain or freely distributable before adding.
